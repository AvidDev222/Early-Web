About us

Hello and welcome!

Robert Glasper is a multi-Grammy and Emmy Award-winning pianist, composer, and producer whose visionary blend of jazz, hip-hop, RnB, and soul has redefined the boundaries of modern music. With an unmatched ability to fuse genres while maintaining emotional depth and technical mastery, Glasper has become one of the most influential voices of his generation. From his groundbreaking work with the Robert Glasper Experiment to collaborations with icons like Kendrick Lamar, Erykah Badu, Herbie Hancock, and Common, Glasper bridges musical worlds with authenticity and innovation. His work spans acclaimed solo albums, film scores, and live performances that challenge conventions and celebrate Black culture in all its richness.
